“Breast milk is the first food for a Baby ,it helps your baby against illness.” – Anonymous

Baby food is any soft easily consumed food other than breastmilk or infant formula that is made specifically for human babies between six months and two years old. The food comes in many varieties and flavors that are purchased ready-made from producers, or it may be table food eaten by the family that has been mashed or otherwise broken down.
As a new parent, you may feel overwhelmed with decision-making, and that includes what food to give your baby. There are so many different feeding styles to pick from and a large amount of baby food options on the market, from jars to pouches to cereals to purees.
